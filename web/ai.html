Artificial Intelligence

Artifical Intelligence is a hot topic. There are alot of research being done as of now to make this a reality and we are coming very close to it. Computer Scientist have been able to program a machine which can analyze data and learn on its own. We have gotten to a point where a machine can collect data and optimize the best possible way to solve a problem.

Intelligent Voice Assistants are one of the example that brings us closer to this reality. Siri, Google Assistant, Amazon Alexa and Microsoft Cortana are couple of artificial intelligence we use on a daily basis.
